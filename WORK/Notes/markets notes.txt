    OPRA --> Options Price Reporting Authority 
    OCC --> means The Options Clearing Corporation

    CUSIP --> Committee on Uniform Security Identification Procedures
    CUSIP number is a nine-character alphanumeric code that uniquely identifies a financial 
    instrument in North America
    --> only used for securities issued in the United States and Canada


    ISIN --> International Securities Identification Number
    is a worldwide standard that uses twelve characters as a unique identifier for any 
    security issued anywhere in the world

    ADR --> American Depositary Receipt
    is a negotiable U.S. dollar-denominated certificate issued by a U.S. bank that 
    represents shares of a foreign company's stock.
